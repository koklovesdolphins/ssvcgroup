Fluoroscopy Phantom

The FLUORO-X-4-150 Phantom Kit is used to check image quality performance of digital and conventional fluoroscopy X-ray systems using single exposure.

These image quality tests are performed on the acceptance test of an X-ray machine to establish a baseline, and on a routine basis. By keeping an on-going record of the test results, it is easy to find any deterioration in performance.

Radiography Phantom
